ABOUT

Hi, I'm Alexis Vorley!

I’m a junior at Davidson College studying Computer Science with a minor in Educational Studies.

I bring an interdisciplinary background across computer science/data science, education, writing, and the arts. I am proficient in Python, Java, and R, with experience in web development, data visualization, and UX design. I have cultivated strong communication and problem-solving skills through roles in research, nonprofits, and teaching.

I’m excited to explore new ways to combine my diverse interests and create a genuine positive impact. I am passionate about fostering inclusion in STEM education, expanding access to the arts, and prioritizing sustainability.
